About The Aazadi Group

The Aazadi Group is a passionate collective based in Belpura, a community where we began our journey through street plays and grassroots activism.

We are dedicated to fighting inequality in society and education, working tirelessly to develop Belpura and create positive change. Our group of 8 committed members operates under the inspiring vision of Dr. B.R. Ambedkar, striving to build a more equitable society.

From humble beginnings in street theater to becoming a force for social change, we believe in the power of education, awareness, and collective action to transform communities.

Our Vision: Following Dr. B.R. Ambedkar's ideals

Our Objectives

Working towards a more equitable society

Fighting for Equality of Opportunity

Ensuring everyone has equal access to opportunities regardless of their background or social status.

Improving Slum Communities

Working to enhance living conditions and infrastructure in underserved communities like Belpura.

Fighting Caste Discrimination

Actively combating caste-based discrimination and promoting social harmony and inclusion.

Promoting Higher Education

Spreading awareness among Dalits about the importance and accessibility of higher education.
