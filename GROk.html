Our Journey

From humble beginnings to a force for change, The Aazadi Group is transforming lives in Belpura and beyond.

2018: The Spark

Started with street plays in Belpura to raise awareness about education.

2020: Growth

Expanded to community programs and workshops under Deeksha Meshram’s leadership.

2023: Impact

Reached over 500 students and launched initiatives for slum improvement.

Our Mission

Education for All

Promoting access to quality education for marginalized communities.

Social Justice

Fighting caste-based discrimination and advocating equality.

Community Development

Improving living conditions in slums like Belpura.
